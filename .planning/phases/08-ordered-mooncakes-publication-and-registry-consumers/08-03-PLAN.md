## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Host/check-out to disposable consumer | Ambient workspaces, credentials, caches, and toolchains must not influence proof. |
| Registry resolution to behavioral execution | Untrusted resolved artifacts must match exact graph and identity before their target results count. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-08-03-01 | Spoofing | dependency source | high | mitigate | New empty home, no workspace/source/Git/path configuration, exact public observations, and contamination scans. |
| T-08-03-02 | Tampering | resolved graph/archive | high | mitigate | Node-for-node graph equality, manifest/index agreement, and downloaded archive SHA-256. |
| T-08-03-03 | Information Disclosure | consumer job | high | mitigate | No secret input, credential file, inherited Moon home, or raw response in proof artifacts. |
| T-08-03-04 | Elevation of Privilege | native execution | medium | mitigate | Pinned toolchain/root, bounded public program, isolated directory, and no repository scripts imported by the consumer. |
| T-08-03-05 | Denial of Service | cold resolution | low | accept | A bounded failed proof stops progression and cannot mutate the registry. |
</threat_model>
