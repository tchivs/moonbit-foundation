<decisions>
D-01: `PngIndexedImage` is owning, immutable, and PNG-only; do not widen `ImageView`, `ImageFormat`, or `ImageEncoder`.
D-02: the indexed wire route is Type-3 depth 8, non-interlaced, RGB-palette, Stored DEFLATE, and filter None.
D-03: validate checked geometry, exact unpacked-index length, palette cardinality, and every index before copied ownership, output, or budget exposure.
D-04: eager framing is IHDR → PLTE → IDAT → IEND and must decode via the existing generic RGB8 path.
D-05: one bounded traversal remains; private frame layout facts may vary but zero-PLTE legacy output stays byte-identical.
D-06: palette alpha, resumable output, low-bit indexed depths, Adam7, quantization, and staging are outside this plan.
</decisions>

<interfaces>
`PngEncoder` currently owns private strategy, filter, interlace, and profile selections; its generic `ImageEncoder::encode` consumes an `ImageView` and constructs `PngEncodeMachine::new_with_profile` before emitting and acknowledging one byte at a time.

`PngEncodeMachine` currently owns the source, profile, deflate state, IHDR/IDAT CRC state, Adler state, and scalar total length. Its `byte_at` hard-codes the no-ancillary layout; its `acknowledge` advances integrity state only after writer acceptance. Extend this seam instead of creating another encoder.

`@bytes.OwnedBytes::from_bytes` is the established defensive ownership mechanism. Public generic decode is available through `@codec.ImageDecoder::decode(PngDecoder::new(), ...)` and canonicalizes Type-3 data to RGB8.
</interfaces>
</context>
