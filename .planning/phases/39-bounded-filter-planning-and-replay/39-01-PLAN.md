  <name>Deterministic PNG method-0 filter arithmetic</name>
  <files>modules/mb-image/png/encode.mbt, modules/mb-image/png/encode_wbtest.mbt</files>
  <behavior>
    - PNGF-02: for RGB8 (`bpp = 3`) and straight-RGBA8 (`bpp = 4`), candidate bytes use original raw samples at left, above, and upper-left positions, with absent neighbors treated as zero.
    - None, Sub, Up, Average, and Paeth each produce byte-modulo residuals; Average performs its addition in a non-byte intermediate and Paeth uses the PNG prescribed nearest-predictor comparison order.
    - Each candidate score is the checked sum of signed-byte residual magnitudes, including magnitude 128 for byte value `0x80`; candidates are visited in None, Sub, Up, Average, Paeth order and only a strictly smaller score replaces the winner.
  </behavior>
</feature>
