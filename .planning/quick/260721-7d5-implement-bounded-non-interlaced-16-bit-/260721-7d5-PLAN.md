<objective>
Implement strict, bounded decoding of non-interlaced 16-bit PNG grayscale (type 0) and truecolour (type 2) into the existing RGB8/RGBA8 image models. Map every big-endian raw 16-bit component to its high byte (`sample >> 8`) explicitly and document that no rounding, scaling, dithering, or colour-management transform occurs.

Purpose: extend the pure-MoonBit eager PNG interchange decoder while retaining its authenticated transport, exact resource bounds, atomic result visibility, arbitrary-IDAT-boundary handling, and four-target evidence.
Output: private 16-bit type-0/type-2 transport and raster support plus independently generated fixtures and portable tests. Excludes 16-bit type 4/6, Adam7, colour management, encoding changes, public streaming, QOI, release, and configuration.
</objective>
