# Quick Task 260721-5j4: Low-Bit Grayscale PNG Summary

**The bounded PNG decoder now expands non-interlaced type-0 samples at depths 1, 2, 4, and 8 from packed filtered rows into exact RGB8 or straight RGBA8 output.**

## Accomplishments

- Added checked packed-row sizing, type-0 depth validation, raw-sample `tRNS` bounds checks, and a single image-plus-two-rows resource reservation.
- Reconstructs PNG filters in two private packed-byte rows with bpp 1, unpacks only actual-width MSB-first samples, scales 1/2/4-bit values, and compares raw samples to the `tRNS` key.
- Added independently reconstructed low-bit fixture families covering all filters, byte crossings, exhaustive two-IDAT splits, transparency, hostile keys, generated tests, and the manifest digest.

colour-management semantics remain future work.
