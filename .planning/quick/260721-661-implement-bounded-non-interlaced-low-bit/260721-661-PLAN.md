<objective>
Implement bounded, non-interlaced indexed PNG decode at bit depths 1, 2, and 4 while retaining the current depth-8 behavior. The decoder must validate PLTE and optional tRNS, filter encoded packed bytes with bpp 1, unpack only real MSB-first samples, and emit RGB8 or straight RGBA8.

Purpose: extend the strict eager PNG interchange decoder without compromising its resource, atomicity, or four-target guarantees.
Output: private decoder support plus generated, independently checked conformance evidence. This excludes release/publishing/configuration, QOI, Adam7, 16-bit, grayscale-alpha, colour management, and public streaming.
</objective>
