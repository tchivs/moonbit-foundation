<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Repository source to prepared artifact | Tracked inputs become the only payload later admitted to the secret-bearing job. |
| Prepare job to publisher job | Cross-job artifact metadata and bytes are untrusted until schema, path, size, digest, and binding revalidation. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-08-01-01 | Tampering | prepared bundle | high | mitigate | Closed paths/roles, canonical manifest, per-payload size/SHA-256, exact source/intent/journal/toolchain bindings, and adversarial tests. |
| T-08-01-02 | Information Disclosure | prepare job/artifact | high | mitigate | Keep credentials and raw live output absent; scan the closed inventory before upload. |
| T-08-01-03 | Spoofing | artifact identity | medium | mitigate | Bind artifact name and manifest to repository, run, source, current intent, and immutable root intent. |
| T-08-01-04 | Denial of Service | malformed inventory | low | accept | Fail closed with bounded local validation; a malformed bundle can stop preparation but cannot mutate the registry. |
</threat_model>
