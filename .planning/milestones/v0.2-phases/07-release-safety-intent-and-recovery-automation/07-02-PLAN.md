<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|---|---|
| Prior workflow artifact -> verified journal state | A replaced, truncated, replayed, or stale artifact could authorize an illegal next mutation. |
| CLI/mutator result -> sanitized registry observation | Raw output may leak secrets, and an ambiguous exit can hide a successful publication. |
| Reducer decision -> live adapter | A controller bypass could execute more than one module or violate dependency order. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| T-07-05 | Tampering | journal chain | high | mitigate | Closed records, sequence and prior digest verification, exact intent/run/artifact binding, and full-chain recomputation. |
| T-07-06 | Tampering/Denial of Service | replay and concurrency | high | mitigate | Pure reducer classifications plus preserved checkpoints and no duplicate mutation command. |
| T-07-07 | Information Disclosure | adapter output | high | mitigate | Allowlisted sanitized observations only; credential home removed before journal/artifact production. |
| T-07-08 | Elevation of Privilege | LiveOneStep adapter | critical | mitigate | Exact preconditions, explicit live switch, one dependency-safe command, workflow isolation in 07-03, and no Required reachability. |
| T-07-09 | Tampering | ambiguous publish outcome | high | mitigate | Mandatory read-only re-observation; absence/exact/mismatch/unknown have separate forward-only dispositions. |
