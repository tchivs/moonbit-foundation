# v0.17 Milestone Audit — GrayAlpha16 PNG Interchange

## Result

**PASSED WITH NON-BLOCKING TECH DEBT.** All four v0.17 requirements are
satisfied, all three phases passed verification, and the cross-phase audit found
no missing or broken connection. The intended user path is wired end to end:
legal little-endian `ImageFormat::graya16()` descriptor → checked owned storage
→ public eager or caller-buffered factory → shared bounded Type-4/16 encoder
→ exact PNG bytes → public decoder canonicalized to straight RGBA8 high bytes.

The format deliberately rejects a big-endian GrayAlpha16 descriptor before PNG
admission. This is an enforced model boundary, not a missing encoder capability.

## End-to-End User Flows

1. **Create, encode, and decode a legal image — COMPLETE.** A user creates a
   packed little-endian U16 GrayAlpha descriptor, writes `34,12,C5,A7` and
   `0F,BE,76,5A` through checked component-byte storage, then calls the public
   eager factory. The emitted scanline is literally
   `00 12 34 A7 C5 BE 0F 5A 76`; public decode returns
   `(12,12,12,A7)` and `(BE,BE,BE,5A)`. The source fixture, wire assertion, and
   decoder assertion are executable in `encode_test.mbt:180-211`, `:478-508`,
   and `:1067-1085`.

2. **Caller-buffered encoding under hostile capacities — COMPLETE.** Each of
   Stored, FixedOrStored, and DynamicOrFixedOrStored crossed with None and
   Adaptive uses a fresh public chunk encoder. Zero-capacity, one-byte, and
   ragged schedules preserve accepted-only accounting, untouched lease tails,
   eager-byte identity, and sticky successful terminals
   (`stream_encode_test.mbt:708-763`, `:1292-1328`).

3. **Failure and mutation safety — COMPLETE.** All six strategy/filter pairs
   reject incompatible, geometry, output, work, and budget failures before
   eager output or caller-lease exposure (`stream_encode_test.mbt:2567-2611`,
   `:2733-2753`). Fixed and Dynamic Adaptive replay detects a post-prefix U16
   mutation before writing another lease byte and remains sticky
   (`:2925-2996`). Big-endian GrayAlpha16 is rejected during descriptor
   construction (`encode_test.mbt:1087-1111`).
