# v0.5 QOI Streaming I/O — Milestone Audit

## Verdict

**PASSED.** All seven v0.5 streaming-QOI requirements are satisfied by the three
verified phases. The public flow is portable and isolated from release,
registry, credential, FFI, PNG/DEFLATE, and publication routes.

## Cross-phase flow

`caller-owned input chunks → QoiStreamDecoder.push → finish() → flip_horizontal → QoiStreamEncoder.pull into caller-owned leases → canonical QOI output`

The existing `qoi-portable` example proves this route on `js`, `wasm`,
`wasm-gc`, and `native`, including fixed schedules, exact byte counters,
canonical output bytes, rolling digest, and SHA-256 evidence.

## Integration and quality evidence

- Six decoder/encoder/example/fixture/quality boundaries are connected; no
  orphaned exports, missing links, or broken flows were found.
- The checked fixture generator, all-target QOI package suite, isolated public
  example, and selected `Qoi` quality lane each pass.
- The QOI lane asserts its exact four-stage trace and traps broader
  qualification, registry, release, publication, credential, and LLVM routes.
